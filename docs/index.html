The Problem

There's a chance of snow and suddenly everyone loses their minds. Schools panic. Parents panic. The grocery store runs out of bread and milk.

The Solution

Snow Hype Meter pulls real-time weather data and runs it through our Hype Engine to tell you if the panic is actually justified.

The Verdict

We don't just give you a number. We give you a verdict you can understand: from "TOTAL DUD" to "ABSOLUTE CHAOS".

Hype Levels

  • 90-100 ABSOLUTE CHAOS — Stay home. Stock up. It's happening.
  • 70-89 SCHOOL CLOSING IMMINENT — Keep your phone charged.
  • 50-69 DELAY POTENTIAL — Could go either way.
  • 30-49 GROCERY RUN — Maybe grab some supplies.
  • 1-29 JUST COLD — Wear a jacket.
  • 0 TOTAL DUD — Go back to bed.
